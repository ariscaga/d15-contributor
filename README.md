# DollarFifteen

## Here, you really can make a dollar out of fifteen cents.

DollarFifteen is the contributor-facing mobile platform powering the human intelligence layer behind modern AI systems.

Built as the public access point for D15, DollarFifteen enables everyday people — especially those historically excluded from the AI economy — to learn, earn, and participate directly in the systems shaping the future of artificial intelligence.

This is not gig work.
This is not exploitative data labor.
This is human intelligence infrastructure.

# MVP Contributor Experience

The DollarFifteen MVP focuses on five core pillars:

## 1. Mobile First

Built for smartphone accessibility from day one.

## 2. Consent + Data Ownership

Contributors understand what they are contributing, how it is used, and what rights they maintain.

## 3. Learn + Earn

Users develop AI literacy while completing paid tasks.

## 4. Trust + Dignity

The platform is designed to treat contributors as infrastructure — not disposable labor.

## 5. Rewards + Growth

Cash earnings, bonus systems, and future pathways for skill expansion.
