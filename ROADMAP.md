## DollarFifteen Contributor MVP Roadmap

### From mobile-first contributor app → human intelligence participation system.

---

# Overview

DollarFifteen is the contributor-facing access layer of D15.

This roadmap outlines how DollarFifteen evolves from:

## Early MVP

→

## Trusted contributor network

→

## Skilled intelligence workforce

→

## Enterprise-ready human intelligence infrastructure

# Core Thesis

DollarFifteen does not begin by trying to build everything.

It begins by solving one critical problem:
